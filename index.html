About Me

I am a graduate of the University of Utah where I studied Psychology, with an emphasis on Human Factors and human-centered design. My interests include healthcare system and product design, Human-Computer interaction, cognitive modeling and its applications in A.I. and more. I have experience in basic engineering and computer programming, and a strong interest in applying my background and passion to the fields of software, engineering, and design.

I've been studying computer science since I attended Cambridge College's summer study program. I have always been interested in Computer Science, and I joined the University of Utah's Full Stack Developer Bootcamp as a Team Lead to pursue this interest. I'd like to be able to apply my new skills to my career in usability and Human Factors, or pursue a position in Development.
